St. Lawrence County Board of Elections

- What is the Board of Elections? Who works there?

The SLC Board of Elections is a bi-partisan Board representative of the two major political parties as determined by the most recent Gubernatorial election.

- Where is the office? When is it open? How can I call or email? - What elections will be held in 2020? When are they? Who can vote in them?

All registered St. Lawrence county voters, regardless of party enrollement, are eligible to vote in that election. 2020 elections include:

  • The presidential primary will be held on Tuesday April 28, 2020
  • The federal, state and local primary will be held on Tuesday June 23, 2020
  • This year's general election will be held on Tuesday November 3, 2020

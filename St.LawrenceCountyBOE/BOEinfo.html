St. Lawrence County Board of Elections

- What is the Board of Elections?

The SLC Board of Elections is a bi-partisan board representative of the two major political parties as determined by the most recent Gubernatorial election.

- Where is the office? When is it open?

Our Canton Board of Elections office is located at the Human Services Building, 80 State Highway 310, Second Floor. Our regular office hours are weekdays, 9AM to 5PM.

- What elections will be held in 2020? When are they? Who can vote in them?

All registered St. Lawrence county voters, regardless of party enrollement, are eligible to vote in that election. 2020 elections include:

  • The presidential primary which will be held on Tuesday April 28, 2020
  • The federal, state and local primary which will be held on Tuesday June 23, 2020
  • This year's general election which will be held on Tuesday November 3, 2020

    - When and where can I go to vote early?

    Early voting will occur at our Canton Board of Elections office in the two weeks before each upcoming special, primary or general election. Pre-registration, which is already available to those of age 17, extends to age 16 effective January, 2020.
